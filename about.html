About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur is a leading center for education, research, and innovation in computing. The department offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs that combine strong theoretical foundations with practical problem-solving skills.

Research in the department spans diverse areas such as artificial intelligence, systems, cybersecurity, data science, software engineering, and theoretical computer science. Supported by modern infrastructure and active collaborations with academia and industry, the department fosters an environment of innovation and interdisciplinary learning.

Through excellence in teaching, research, and societal impact, the department continues to contribute to the advancement of computing and the development of future technology leaders.
