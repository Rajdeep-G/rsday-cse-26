🗺️ Travel & Logistics
📍 Reaching IIT Kharagpur
The campus is ~2 km from Kharagpur Railway Station. Trains from Howrah (Kolkata) take about 2–2.5 hrs. From Kolkata Airport, take a taxi/metro to Howrah, then a train to Kharagpur.
🚖 Station → IIT Campus
Head to the Main Gate ("PURI Gate") via:
  • Shared or reserved auto / toto
  • Ola / Rapido bike services
Takes 10–15 min. The CSE Dept is a 7–8 min walk from the gate.
🏨 Accommodation
Available (Feb 27 night) on a twin-sharing basis for those who requested it. (Standard bedding will be provided at the halls. Please bring your personal belongings.)
  • Male: B. R. Ambedkar Hall
  • Female: Zakir Hussain Hall
🍽️ Food & Facilities
  • Tech Market – daily needs & affordable eateries
  • B. C. Roy Hospital – on-campus medical facility
  • Restaurants: Calcutta 64, Dreamland, Heritage, PFC
  • Fast food: KFC, Wow Momo, Domino's, Tea Junction
🛵 Getting Around Campus
  • Toto (e-rickshaw) & Rapido bike services
  • Most academic venues are easily walkable
🏫 Venues & lunch → CSE: ~5 min walk
🏠 Hostels → Venue: ~20 min walk
